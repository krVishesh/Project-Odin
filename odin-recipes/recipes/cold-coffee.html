Cold Coffee

Ingredients needed:

  • Instant coffee
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Whipped cream or chocolate syrup (optional)

Steps to follow:

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee.
  2. Add sugar and milk to taste.
  3. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Add ice cubes to a glass.
  5. Pour the coffee over the ice cubes.
  6. Garnish with whipped cream or chocolate syrup (optional).
  7. Enjoy your refreshing cold coffee!
