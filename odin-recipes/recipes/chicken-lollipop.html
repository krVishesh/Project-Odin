Chicken Lollipop

Ingredients needed:

  • Chicken wings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Garlic powder
  • Ginger powder
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili sauce
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Steps to follow:

  1. Cut the chicken wings into lollipop shape.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, garlic powder, ginger powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the egg.
  4. Dip the chicken lollipops in the beaten egg.
  5. Coat the chicken lollipops with the flour mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  7. Fry the chicken lollipops until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. In a separate bowl, mix soy sauce, chili sauce, garlic powder, and ginger powder.
  9. Toss the fried chicken lollipops in the sauce mixture.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
