Noodles

Ingredients needed:

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Sauce or seasoning

Steps to follow:

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Cook noodles for the recommended time.
  4. Drain the water from the noodles.
  5. Add your favorite sauce or seasoning.
  6. Mix well and serve hot.
